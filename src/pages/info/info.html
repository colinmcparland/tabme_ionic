Whats the purpose of this app?

This app is designed to help you track and retain your money across friends.

Ever had to cover someones meal because they forgot their wallet? Then forgot about it as time goes on?

How about going back and forth with a friend and forgetting how much you owe eachother?

No more with Tab.me!

How do I use the app?

Add an IOU

This page allows you to add an IOU from a friend. Simply select a friend by their email, input the amount they owe you and the record will show up on both of your apps.

From this page, you can send reminders for an IOU and waive an IOU is it has been repaid.

View my IOUs

This page allows you to see all your IOUs, including the ones you owe and the ones owed to you.

How do I use the app? (Continued)

Add a Friend

This allows you to add a friend by their email address. Note a friend must be registered with Tab.me before you can start adding their IOUs

Logout

Logs you out of the app. You can login to your account from any device using your email and chosen password.

You're all set to use the app! Go start adding friends and keeping track of your hard-earned money!
